Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how The Quran App ("the app") handles information. The app is designed to work primarily offline and does not require account creation.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or precise location through the app itself.

The app stores reading-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • bookmarks
  • reading position (last page)
  • settings and preferences
  • highlight data

This local data stays on your device unless you delete the app/device data.

Internet Access

The app may use internet access to download optional Quran page image packages ("Mushaf assets") from official release links so you can read them offline later. These downloads are handled by standard Android system/network components.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

The app does not require sign-in.

If the app is distributed through Google Play, Google may process technical and diagnostic data under its own policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share personal data with third parties because the app does not directly collect personal user profiles.

Data Retention and Deletion

User data created in the app (bookmarks, highlights, settings, downloaded assets) is kept on your device until you remove it.

You can delete this data by:

  • clearing app storage in Android settings, or
  • uninstalling the app

Children

The app is general-audience religious/educational software and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to an offline-first app design. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the latest revision.
